Sausage Balls

Image of sausage balls from pexels sitting on a dinner plate ready to be eaten.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork sausage, at room temperature
  • 2 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 12 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded

Preperation Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Combine room temperature sausage, biscuit mix, and shredded Cheddar together in a large bowl; mix with your hands until well combined. The mixture may seem dry at first, but keep mixing until it comes together evenly.
  3. Roll mixture into about 30 walnut-sized balls; transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and sausage is cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes.
  5. That's it! Enjoy your Sausage Balls!
